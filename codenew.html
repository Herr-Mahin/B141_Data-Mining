Key Insights:
1. Age and category are significant predictors of the total sales price.
2. Female customers tend to spend more on average compared to male customers.
3. Shoes and clothing categories have higher sales volumes.
Recommendations:
1. Focus marketing efforts on high-value customer segments (e.g., customers aged 30-50).
2. Introduce more promotions and discounts in high-value categories like shoes and clothing.
3. Use targeted advertising for customers who prefer credit card payments.
